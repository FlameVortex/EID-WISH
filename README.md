## 🎯 Overview

**Eid Wish for Someone Special** is a beautifully crafted interactive experience that lets you send personalized Eid greetings in a unique and memorable way.
Simply share the link with your special person and make their Eid even more magical.✨

### Key Highlights

- 💫 **Smooth Animations** 
- 📱 **Responsive Design**
- ⚡ **High Performance**
- 🎀 **Modern UI** 

---


### How to Use
1. Visit the website
2. Click **"Enter ✨"** to begin
3. Tap **"Tap for Magic ✨"** to reveal each wish card
4. Enjoy the special surprise at the end with floating hearts!
